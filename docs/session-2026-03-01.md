## 📝 Technical Decisions Made

### 1. Smart Switches vs Shelly Relays

**Original plan:** Shelly Plus 1 Mini behind existing switches
- Pros: Keep existing switch appearance
- Cons: Complex installation, WAGO connectors, tight boxes

**New plan:** Smart switches replacing physical switches
- Pros: Simple installation, future-proof, easy maintenance
- Cons: Changes switch appearance (but family okay with it)
- **Winner:** Smart switches - much better for skill level

### 2. Water Meter Validation Settings

**Problem:** "Rate too high" errors blocking readings
- Device had old incorrect value stored
- Maximum Rate Value was too strict (0.05 = 50 liters)

**Solution:**
- Increased Maximum Rate Value to 999999 (temporarily)
- Kept "Skip Messages on Error" enabled
- Let auto-training improve accuracy naturally
- **Result:** 90%+ accuracy achieved after 24-48 hours

### 3. Ring Doorbell Revival Method

**Problem:** Deep discharge lockout, standard chargers too weak

**Solution discovered:**
- Power bank provides 2A+ current (vs 0.5-1A wall chargers)
- Higher current wakes deeply discharged battery
- Once battery at 20-30%, hardwired power takes over
- **Method works!** Battery protection circuit reset

### Electrical Wiring Discovered

**Switch box contents:**
- 2 × Brown wires (Live in/out - loop)
- 1 × Black wire (Switched live to light)
- 2 × Blue wires (Neutral - capped)
- 2 × Green/Yellow (Ground - capped)

**Why smart switches are simpler:**
```
OLD SWITCH (2 wires):          NEW SMART SWITCH (3 wires):
TOP: Black (to light)          L-out: Black (to light)
BOTTOM: 2 Browns (power)       L-in: 2 Browns (power)
                               N: 2 Blues (neutral - uncap)

No WAGO connectors needed!
Just direct wire connections!
```

## 🎓 Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well

1. **Power bank solution for Ring doorbell**
   - Deep discharge needs high current (2A+)
   - Wall chargers often insufficient (0.5-1A)
   - Power banks provide 2A+ reliably

2. **AI-on-the-Edge water meter**
   - Purpose-built firmware extremely reliable
   - OCR on-device reduces HA load
   - 90%+ accuracy achievable with training
   - MQTT integration seamless

3. **Pivoting from Shelly relays to smart switches**
   - Research before buying saves time
   - Sometimes "cheaper" costs more (complexity)
   - €5-10 extra per switch worth the simplicity
   - Test small before committing to full rollout

### What to Improve

1. **Test installations before bulk ordering**
   - Ordered Shelly Plus 1 Mini before fully understanding complexity
   - Should have tested 1 unit first
   - Learned: Always test phase with 2-3 units

2. **Battery maintenance**
   - Ring doorbell shouldn't have sat dead for 10 days
   - Deep discharge damages lithium batteries
   - Learned: Check battery levels weekly

3. **Documentation as you go**
   - Some decisions weren't documented immediately
   - Created guides retroactively
   - Better: Document decisions in real-time
